16 Swarasthanas — Complete Reference

All 16 tonal positions with your notation standard, phonetic AI encoding, and Carnot symbols.

🎹 MANDRA STHAYI (Lower)

Prefix: .s .r .g .m .p .d .n
Lower octave — about 1 octave below Madhya.

🎼 MADHYA STHAYI (Middle)

Prefix: S R G M P D N
The home octave — most compositions are written here.

🎵 TARA STHAYI (Upper)

Prefix: S' R' G' M' P' D' N'
Upper octave — soprano range.

15 Gamakas — Complete AI Encoding

All 15 classical gamakas from Sangita Sampradaya Pradarshini with notation symbols, AI tokens, and pitch curve types.

~~
1. Kampita (कम्पित)
Vibrato oscillation between adjacent svaras. Like Western vibrato.
GMK_KAMP · curve_type: 3 (oscillate)
Vocal + Veena
~^~
2. Andola (आन्दोल)
Wide slow oscillation. Pitch meanders between neighbours — target svara not sounded directly.
GMK_ANDO · curve_type: 4 (wide_oscillate)
Vocal
(^)
3. Sphurita (स्फुरित)
Grace note from above — touch upper svara, land on target. Used in arohana.
GMK_SPHU · curve_type: 5 (grace_up)
Both
(v)
4. Pratyaghata (प्रत्याघात)
Reverse grace — touch lower svara, land on target. Used in avarohana.
GMK_PRAT · curve_type: 6 (grace_down)
Both
*
5. Nokku (நொக்கு)
Stress accent — sudden forceful articulation of a note. Attack emphasis.
GMK_NOKK · curve_type: 7 (accent)
Veena
^
6. Etra Jaru (ஏற்ற ஜரு)
Upward glide / portamento — slides from a lower pitch up to the target svara.
GMK_EJRU · curve_type: 1 (linear_up)
Both
v
7. Irakka Jaru (இறக்க ஜரு)
Downward glide / portamento — slides from a higher pitch down to the target svara.
GMK_IJRU · curve_type: 2 (linear_down)
Both
{or}
8. Orikkai (ஒரிக்கை)
Subtle flick/ornament — primarily a veena left-hand technique.
GMK_ORIK · curve_type: 9 (complex)
Veena only
{ot}
9. Othukkal (ஒதுக்கல்)
Pushing/pressing ornament — note pushed and held against string.
GMK_OTHU · curve_type: 9 (complex)
Veena only
{tr}
10. Tribhinna (त्रिभिन्न)
Three-string technique on veena — flattened finger pressed on three strings simultaneously.
GMK_TRIB · curve_type: 9 (complex)
Veena only
{mu}
11. Mudrita (मुद्रित)
Mouth-closed ornament — muffled, nasal sound. Vocal music only.
GMK_MUDR · curve_type: 9 (complex)
Vocal only
{nm}
12. Namita (नमित)
Decreasing volume ornament — gentle fading of a note to near silence.
GMK_NAMI · curve_type: 8 (fade)
Both
{mx}
13. Misrita (मिश्रित)
Mixed gamaka — a blend of two gamaka types applied to one note.
GMK_MISR · curve_type: 9 (complex)
Both
=
14. Ahata (आहत)
Direct struck note — full attack, no embellishment. The svara as written.
GMK_AHAT · curve_type: 0 (flat)
Both
{ul}
15. Ullasita (उल्लसित)
Sparkling ornament — rapid mordent-like movement with joyful character.
GMK_ULLA · curve_type: 9 (complex)
Both
